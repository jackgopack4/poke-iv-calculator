How to use this

1. Pick the species. 2. Set the nature (if known — check the summary screen in‑game). 3. Enter the level and the six stats as shown on the in‑game stat screen. 4. Results appear instantly.

Narrowing IVs: at low levels several IVs can produce the same stat. To narrow, level up your Pokémon and click Add another level to enter its new stats. The calculator intersects possibilities across all rows.

EVs: for untrained Pokémon (freshly caught / hatched with no battles) leave the EV toggle off. If your Pokémon has fought anything, toggle it on and enter the EVs you've gained in each stat (4 EVs per 1 stat point at level 100).

Characteristic: shown in the summary screen, this limits one stat's IV to 5 specific values (the highest IV stat, with ties broken by a random order). It's very useful — use it when known.

Offline use: save this file to your iPhone via Safari → Share → Save to Files. Works forever without internet.
